About Us

Mission

At English Plus, our mission is to provide world-class English language education, empowering individuals to reach their highest potential and excel in the global community.

Vision

Our vision is to become a premier English language school known for its commitment to excellence, innovation, and comprehensive language education.

Core Values

Our core values shape every interaction and course we offer:

Quality

We ensure that each course meets the highest educational standards.

Integrity

We uphold honesty and transparency in everything we do.

Student Success

Every student's success is our priority and the driving force behind our teaching.

Global Perspective

We aim to provide a globally-minded education, enhancing students' cultural awareness.

Our Philosophy

Our experienced and passionate educators are committed to helping each student achieve their English language goals. With expertise in various teaching methodologies, our team offers high-quality instruction, continuous support, and individualized attention.

Our Courses

English Plus offers programs tailored to every age group, skill level, and learning objective. Each course is designed to foster language skills and cultural understanding, preparing students for academic and professional success.

For Kids (3-7 years old)

Course: Fun Tots

Objective

To introduce young learners to English through fun, playful activities.

Outcomes
  • Develop basic listening and speaking skills.
  • Learn simple vocabulary and phrases.
  • Build confidence in using English.
Achievements
  • RespoChildren respond to basic commands and questions
  • Children sing simple English songs and rhymes.
  • Children engage in basic English conversations with ease.

For Kids (8-12 years old)

Course: Junior Explorers

Objective

Enhance English skills through interactive and engaging activities.

Outcomes
  • Strengthen reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills.
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.
  • Gain cultural awareness and global perspective.
  • Use grammar and vocabulary correctly.
Achievements
  • Children read and understand simple texts.
  • Children write coherent sentences and paragraphs.
  • Children engage in conversations on familiar topics using proper grammar.

For Teens (13-18 years old)

Course: Teen Achievers

Objective
  • Level 1: Develop foundational English language skills.
  • Level 2: Enhance fluency and accuracy.
  • Level 3: Master advanced language skills for academic success.
  • Level 4: Achieve expert-level English proficiency.
Achievements
  • Level 1: Communicate basic needs and ideas.
  • Level 2: Participate in everyday conversations and write simple texts.
  • Level 3: Understand and produce complex written texts.
  • Level 4: Communicate fluently and accurately in professional and academic contexts.

Course: IELTS Preparation

Objective

Equip students with the skills and confidence to achieve their target IELTS band score.

Outcomes
  • Master essential IELTS exam strategies.
  • Improve reading, writing, listening, and speaking.
  • Boost confidence for the IELTS exam.
Achievements
  • Understand complex academic texts and lectures.
  • Write structured academic essays and reports.
  • Speak fluently on a range of topics.
  • Reach desired IELTS band scores.

Online Learning

Online Kids Classes: Interactive, engaging sessions designed for young learners to improve listening, speaking, and vocabulary through online learning platforms.

Outcomes

Develop essential language skills for online communication.

Achievements

Children actively participate and complete assignments independently.

Online IELTS Prep: A flexible, accessible option for those preparing for the IELTS exam.

Outcomes

Familiarize with IELTS strategies and build confidence.

Achievements

Students effectively analyze performance, apply exam strategies, and achieve target scores.
